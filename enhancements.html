Consumables / Potions

  • Use Flask of the Seventh Demon as your flask buff. Boosts primary stat.
  • Eat Barracuda Mrglgagh to gain the mastery buff it provides, as well as to regain health when out of combat.
  • Use Ancient Healing Poition for immediate healing if you do not have Healthstones.

Gems / Sockets

  • Use Saber's Eye of Agility in all socket slots to be maximum baller.
  • Use Masterful ShadowRuby as a substitute if you are poor and bad at the game.
