Welcome to the Enhancement Shaman Guide Overview. This guide has been written by none other than xXxShaManThugLyfexXx himself. xXxShaManThugLyfexXx is known to be the top shaman in all of World of Warcraft and as such, his word should not be doubted.

Enhancement Shamans are crazy men wielding dual weapons in order to hit things. They harness the power of the elements to destroy their enemies, much like Aang, from Avatar the Last Airbender. Shaman's are known to have very ugly faces and will wear masks to cover up. Carving up logs is essential to the culture of these people.

Changes in Patch 7.1.5

Spell Changes
  • Lava Lash - Damage has been raised from 20% to 999% of weapon damage.
  • Rockbiter - Maestrom generated per cast has been raised from 15 to 40.
  • Lightning Bolt - Damage reduced to 2% down from 30% of Spell Power.
Talent Changes
  • Crashing Storm - now deals 200% of Attack Power up from 64%.
  • Rainfall - Healing increased to 95% of Spell Power up from 40%.
  • Earthen Spike - Maelstrom cost reduced to 20 down from 60, and damage increased to 1900% Attack Power up from 500%.
Basic Information
  • Although these reworks may look drastic, these were much needed changes to balance the class and improve quality of life...kappa.
  • Ability priority still revolves around: Stormstrike,Windfury, Lava Lash, Feral Spirit.
