Enhancement Shaman: Ability Rotation


General Information


On this section of the guide, we'll be discussing the core abilities which make up the Enhancement Shaman class. For the most optimal performance, a sequence of steps to use your abilities will be given to repeat. This is called a Rotation. Following down the list, we will go from highest priority to be followed, down to the lowest priority instruction.


Ability Priority List

  1. Cast Rockbiter to generate resources(Maelstrom) and maintain Landslide buff for increased effect duration.
  2. Cast Feral Spirit on cooldown. Immediately cast Crash Lightning to activate Alpha Wolf trait if you have learned this ability.
  3. Maintain the Flametongue buff at all times.
  4. Cast Lightning Bolt if you have 50+ Maelstrom.
  5. Cast Stormstrike whenever available to make use of Stormbringer procs.
  6. Cast Lava Lash whenever you reach 120 Maelstrom to prevent resource waste.


Explanation
  • With this simple and optimal rotation of only 6 buttons, there is less chance for error which will negatively impact performance.
  • This setup is clearly the best because I said so.
